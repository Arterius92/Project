Our history

Unlimited Media was founded in 2008 as a video postproduction and media processing facility. At first, the main business was DVD mastering.

2009 – opened CD/DVD store dvd24.ee
2010 – the first VOD operator contract concluded in Estonia with a catalogue of 12 titles
2011 – VOD distribution expanded to Latvian and Lithuanian VOD operators
2011-2014 – catalogue expanded to over 100 titles
2015 – VOD reach was around 2M screens in the Baltic countries
2016 – 500k total annual VOD views mark
2017 – 1M total annual VOD views mark
2018 – 3,8M total annual VOD views Launched a video portal cineve.ee (Estonia)
2019 – 6,25M total annual VOD views
2020 – 4M views in first 6 months. All new cineve.ee vide oportal launched in Estonia, Latvia and Lithiania
